### 1. **Dashboard Overview**
- The dashboard shows you a quick summary of your business:
  - Total customers
  - Total brands
  - Total sales
  - Recent sales activity
  - Low stock alerts

### 2. **Daily Workflow**

#### **Adding a New Sale (Most Common Task)**
1. Click **"Sales"** in the sidebar
2. Click **"Add New Sale"** button (green button at top right)
3. **Search for Customer**: Start typing the customer's name - the system will find them automatically
4. **Select Brand**: Type the oil brand name
5. **Enter Details**:
   - Quantity: How many units sold
   - Price: Total price for this sale
   - Sale Date: Usually today's date (auto-filled)
   - Payment Status: Check "Paid" if customer paid, leave "Unpaid" if they'll pay later
6. **Two Options**:
   - **"Save"**: Just saves the sale
   - **"Save and Print"**: Saves and automatically opens receipt for printing

#### **Adding New Customer**
1. Click **"Customers"** in sidebar
2. Click **"Add New Customer"** button
3. Fill in:
   - Name (required)
   - Phone (optional but helpful)
   - Email (optional)
   - Address (optional)
4. Click **"Save Customer"**

#### **Adding Inventory (Stock)**
1. Click **"Inventory"** in sidebar
2. Click **"Add Inventory"** button
3. Select brand (type to search)
4. Enter quantity
5. Click **"Save Inventory"**

#### **Viewing Customer History**
1. Click **"Customers"** in sidebar
2. Find the customer (use search box at top)
3. Click the **eye icon** üëÅÔ∏è to view their purchase history
4. You'll see all their past purchases, total spent, etc.

### 3. **Reports**

#### **Customer Sales Report**
1. Click **"Reports"** ‚Üí **"Customer Report"**
2. **IMPORTANT**: First select a customer (type their name in the search box)
3. Adjust date range if needed (defaults to current month)
4. Filter by payment status (Paid/Unpaid/All)
5. Click **"Generate Report"**
6. View totals at bottom:
   - Total Paid
   - Total Unpaid
7. Click **"Export Excel"** to download report

### 4. **Tips for Easy Use**

#### **Search Features**
- **Customer Search**: On any page with customer dropdown, just start typing - no need to scroll through long lists
- **Brand Search**: Same as customer - type to find quickly
- **Listing Pages**: Use the search box at top to filter customers, brands, etc.

#### **Quick Actions**
- **From Customer List**: Click the shopping cart icon üõí to quickly add a sale for that customer
- **Receipt Printing**: 
  - Click "Get Receipt" button on any sale details page
  - Or use "Save and Print" when creating new sale
  - Receipt opens in new window - click print button or press Ctrl+P

#### **Understanding Payment Status**
- **Paid**: Customer has paid for this sale
- **Unpaid**: Customer still owes money
- You can change this later by editing the sale

### 5. **Common Tasks**

#### **Check Low Stock**
- Dashboard shows low stock items automatically
- Red badge means urgent - need to restock soon

#### **Edit a Sale**
1. Go to Sales page
2. Find the sale (use date filters if needed)
3. Click **"View"** (eye icon)
4. Click **"Edit"** button
5. Make changes
6. Click **"Update Sale"**

#### **Delete Something**
- Click delete button (trash icon)
- System will ask for confirmation
- **Note**: Deleted items are kept in system but hidden (can be recovered if needed)

### 6. **Best Practices**

1. **Always Select Customer First** in reports - otherwise you'll see a message asking you to select
2. **Check Stock Before Sale** - system shows available stock when selecting brand
3. **Use Search Instead of Scrolling** - much faster for long lists
4. **Print Receipts Immediately** - helps with record keeping
5. **Update Payment Status Regularly** - mark sales as "Paid" when customer pays

### 7. **Troubleshooting**

#### **"Please select a customer to show report"**
- Solution: Type customer name in the search box first, then click "Generate Report"

#### **"Insufficient stock" error**
- Solution: Add more inventory for that brand first, then try the sale again

#### **Can't find a customer**
- Solution: Use the search box - it searches by name, phone, or email

#### **Receipt not printing**
- Solution: Make sure your printer is connected and set as default. Try clicking the print button on the receipt page.

### 8. **Keyboard Shortcuts**
- **Ctrl + P**: Print current page/receipt
- **Tab**: Move to next field (faster than clicking)
- **Enter**: Submit form (when on last field)

### 9. **Mobile/Tablet Use**

- All features work the same way

### 10. **Getting Help**
